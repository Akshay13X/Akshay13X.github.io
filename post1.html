Why It Matters

Medication adherence is vital for achieving the intended outcomes of therapy. Skipping doses or not following the prescribed schedule can lead to treatment failure, complications, or hospitalization.

Pharmacists play a crucial role in helping patients understand their medications and stay on track through counseling and regular check-ins.

Always ask your pharmacist if you have any doubts or if you experience side effects. We are here to help you achieve the best possible health outcomes.
