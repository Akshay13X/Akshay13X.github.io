How Vaccines Protect You

Vaccines stimulate your immune system to recognize and fight off harmful pathogens like viruses and bacteria. They are a safe and effective way to prevent diseases that can cause severe illness or death.

Role of the Pharmacist

Pharmacists can administer vaccines and educate patients on the importance of immunization schedules. Protect yourself and your loved ones by staying updated with your vaccines.
