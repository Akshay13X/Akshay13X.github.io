What is Hypertension?

Hypertension, also known as high blood pressure, is a condition where the force of the blood against the artery walls is consistently too high. It often develops over years and can lead to serious health issues like heart disease or stroke if left uncontrolled.

Role of Pharmacy Care

Pharmacists are vital in hypertension management by offering medication counseling, blood pressure monitoring, and lifestyle advice. Early detection through pharmacy screenings can prevent complications and promote healthier living.
